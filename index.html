JBGODS – Terms & Conditions & Privacy Policy

Last updated: 04/07/2026

1. Acceptance of Terms

By creating an account and using this app, you agree to these Terms & Conditions and this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please stop using the app.

2. Who Can Use the App

Users approved by the admin may view content and use the available features.

Only authorized admins can send messages where admin-only messaging applies.

A Master Admin may remove admins or users who violate rules.

Users may also report abusive messages or content.

3. Payment Plans and What They Provide

The app includes three types of payments:

  • Skating Rink Owner Monthly Subscription
    Unlocks all rink-owner features for 30 days. Access ends automatically after 30 days. This plan does not renew by itself.
  • Annual JBGODS ROSE Awards Membership
    Allows the user to cast votes for the JBGODS Rose Awards.
    This does not unlock extra digital features inside the app.
  • Event Registration Fees
    Confirms participation in real-world skating events.
    Does not unlock digital features inside the app except for the event pass generated after successful registration.

4. Refund Policy

  • Monthly access payments follow the refund rules of the app store used during purchase.
  • Annual membership and event fees are normally non-refundable unless cancelled by the rink or organizer.
  • Refunds for physical services must be handled directly with rink management or the event organizer, where applicable.

5. Correct Information

Please make sure your details are accurate while making payments and using the app. You may be asked to show your confirmation or event pass when visiting the rink or attending events.

6. Access After Payment

  • Monthly access features unlock inside the app after successful payment.
  • Users can vote anytime after completing the Annual ROSE Awards Membership payment.
  • Event registrations may generate a digital event pass inside the app after successful payment.

7. Misuse

Sharing your account, using someone else’s payment without authorization, sharing event passes, or abusing rink claim features may result in your access being removed. Abusive behavior or violation of community rules can also lead to suspension.

8. Community Guidelines (Admins)

We maintain a zero-tolerance policy for objectionable or abusive content.

Admins must not post content that is offensive, abusive, discriminatory, hateful, threatening, illegal, spam, or violates privacy or intellectual property rights.

Users may report inappropriate content. All reports are reviewed by the Master Admin.

The Master Admin may remove or demote admins who violate rules.

Issues are acted upon within 24 hours by removing offending content and restricting access if needed.
