JB GODS – Terms & Conditions & Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 19, 2025

1. Acceptance of Terms

By creating an account and using this app, you agree to these Terms & Conditions and this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

2. Who Can Use the App

Users who become members on admin approval may view content and read admin messages. Only authorized admins may send messages. A Master Admin may remove admins and users who violate these rules.

3. Community Guidelines (Admins)

Admins must not post content that is offensive/abusive, discriminatory/hateful, threatening, illegal, spam, or that violates privacy or intellectual property rights. The Master Admin may remove admins who violate these rules. Users can report concerns at support@jbgods.com.

4. Information We Collect

  • Account details: username, name, email, password.
  • Location data: only if you choose to share it (see Section 5).
  • Usage data: device type, app activity, crash logs (to improve reliability and security).

5. Location Feature (Your Choice)

The app allows you to control who can see your location:

  1. Admins only – your location is visible only to authorized admin accounts.
  2. Everyone – your location is visible to all registered users of the app (not public on the internet).
  3. Off – you can stop sharing your location at any time.
  • You can change this choice at any time in the profile options or in your device’s system settings.
  • We only use your location to display your approximate area as you choose.
  • Location is never sold to third parties.
  • Location data is stored securely and retained only as long as necessary to provide the feature.
iOS permission text (suggested):
“Location is used to show your approximate area. You may choose to share it with admins only, with everyone, or stop sharing at any time.”

6. How We Use Information

  • Create and manage accounts.
  • Provide admin chat features and location-based functionality you enable.
  • Improve reliability, security, and support.

7. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your data. We may share limited data with service providers strictly to operate the app (e.g., Firebase Authentication, Firestore, Analytics, and our map/geolocation provider). These providers must comply with applicable data protection laws and use the data only to provide their services to us.

8. Data Storage & Security

Data is stored securely in cloud services (e.g., Firebase), encrypted in transit and at rest. We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the service or until you request deletion. We use reasonable safeguards to prevent unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

9. Your Rights & Choices

  • Update or delete profile info from within the app.
  • Request account deletion from the Profile menu or by contacting us.
  • Manage permissions (including Location) in your device settings.

10. Contact Us

For questions or requests, contact: support@jbgods.com

This policy may be updated as our services evolve. We will notify you of any material changes within the app.
